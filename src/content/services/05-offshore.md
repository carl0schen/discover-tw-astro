Taiwan's offshore islands offer a tranquil escape from the mainland's pace. Penghu's windswept archipelago features pristine beaches and stone settlements. Green Island and Orchid Island showcase vibrant coral reefs and indigenous Tao culture. Kinmen and Matsu, once military outposts, now charm visitors with their well-preserved wartime architecture and traditional villages. Each island combines natural beauty with distinct cultural heritage, providing authentic experiences away from typical tourist paths.
