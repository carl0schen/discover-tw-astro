Taiwan offers remarkable eco-tourism opportunities despite its compact size. The island features diverse ecosystems from coral reefs to alpine forests. Visitors can explore Taroko Gorge’s marble cliffs, encounter diverse wildlife in Yushan National Park, or witness butterfly migrations in Maolin. Kenting's tropical waters host vibrant coral communities, while Yangmingshan features volcanic landscapes and seasonal flower blooms. Taiwan's rich biodiversity thrives thanks to conservation efforts, allowing visitors to appreciate native species and their habitats through well-maintained trail systems.
