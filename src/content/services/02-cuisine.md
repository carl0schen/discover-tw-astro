Taiwan's food journey captivates with incredible diversity. Savor aboriginal dishes with mountain herbs and millet wine in eastern townships. Explore Hakka cuisine featuring preserved vegetables and lei cha (ground tea). Sample Japanese-influenced sushi with Taiwanese twists. Indulge in desserts like mango shaved ice and pineapple cakes. Beyond famous night markets, discover farm-to-table restaurants, coastal seafood shacks serving just-caught fish, and teahouses offering premium oolong with traditional pastries. Each bite tells a story of Taiwan's multicultural heritage.
