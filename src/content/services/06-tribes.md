Taiwan's indigenous village tours offer authentic cultural experiences in spectacular settings. Visitors can explore Amis villages on the east coast, known for fishing traditions and harvest festivals. Central mountain Atayal communities showcase weaving and facial tattooing history. Bunun villages feature polyphonic singing, while southern Paiwan settlements display intricate woodcarvings and slate houses. Rukai and Tao (Yami) communities present unique cultural practices from millet farming to traditional boat building. Each tribe provides a valuable window into Taiwan's rich indigenous heritage.
