In the long history, prehistoric people, aborigines, Dutch, Spaniards, Japanese, and Han Chinese have all lived in Taiwan, and together they have created a rich and diversified Taiwanese culture, which has given birth to different local people and history and culture, and also left behind a lot of relics of their lives. Walking into Taiwan, you will be able to deeply feel the beauty of the multifaceted cultural monuments of this beautiful island!
