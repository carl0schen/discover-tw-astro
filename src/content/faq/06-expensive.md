---
title: "Is traveling in Taiwan expensive?"
order: 6
---

Taiwan offers exceptional value compared to many other Asian destinations. Budget travelers can comfortably explore Taiwan on $80–100 USD per day, covering accommodation in clean hostels ($20–50 per night), street food meals ($2–5 each), and efficient public transportation ($0.60–2 per ride).

For mid-range travelers, a daily budget of $100–150 USD allows for stays in comfortable hotels, meals at sit-down restaurants ($8–15 per meal), and participation in guided tours.

Many attractions—like night markets, temples, and parks—are either free or very affordable. Museum entry fees typically range from $3–10 USD, while taxi fares start at around $3 USD. Even luxury experiences in Taiwan tend to be much more affordable than in Japan, South Korea, or Western countries.
