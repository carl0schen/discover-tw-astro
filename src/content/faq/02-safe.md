---
title: "Is Taiwan safe for foreign tourists?"
order: 2
---

Taiwan is widely considered one of the safest countries in the world. Violent crime is extremely rare, and locals are generally honest and helpful to international visitors.

* **Personal Safety:** It is generally safe to walk around cities at night. Most streets are well-lit, covered by surveillance cameras, and busy with activity thanks to 24-hour convenience stores.

* **Theft & Scams:** Pickpocketing is uncommon, even in crowded places like night markets, but standard precautions are still recommended. Be mindful of your belongings and cautious of overly persistent strangers in major tourist hubs.

* **Solo Travel:** Taiwan is an excellent destination for solo travelers, including women. Harassment is rare, and the environment is welcoming.

In short, while no place is completely crime-free, travelers seldom face safety issues in Taiwan beyond the need for common sense.
