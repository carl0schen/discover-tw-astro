---
title: "Is it safe to eat street food in Taiwan?"
order: 5
---

Absolutely! Taiwan's street food is renowned globally, with high standards of hygiene and freshness. Night markets like Shilin, Raohe, and Keelung offer incredible variety at reasonable prices. Must-tries include stinky tofu, oyster omelets, bubble tea, and gua bao (Taiwanese hamburgers). Even sensitive travelers rarely experience digestive issues.
