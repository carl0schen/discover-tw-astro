Satoshi Tsumabuki once again delves into Taiwan to explore his own Taiwanese "favorites"! The camera follows him as he visits the cultural landmark of the Dragon and Tiger Pagodas in Kaohsiung, savors the refreshing taste of a traditional ice and fruit shop, takes in the artistic atmosphere of the Chi Mei Museum in Tainan, and enjoys a shopping experience at Sanfong Jhong Street. Each setting showcases Taiwan's unique charm. The behind-the-scenes footage captures his genuine smiles and fun moments, inviting the audience to join him on this journey through a wonderfully diverse Taiwan—his "favorites" Taiwan trip.
